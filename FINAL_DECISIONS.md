# 🧺 INVENTORY SYSTEM (PREMIUM+ ONLY)

## What Farmer Can Do
- ✅ View available products (feed, medicine, etc.)
- ✅ See price (if dairy allows)
- ✅ Send product request (name, quantity, message)

## What Farmer CANNOT Do
- ❌ Place automatic order
- ❌ Pay online
- ❌ Reduce stock directly

**👉 It's a REQUEST, not an ORDER.**

## What Dairy Can Do
- ✅ Accept or reject request
- ✅ Adjust quantity or price
- ✅ Mark as sold (cash / credit)
- ✅ Auto-update stock
- ✅ Auto-add to farmer ledger
- ✅ Include in next milk bill

## What Inventory Does NOT Have
- ❌ No delivery system
- ❌ No warehouse management
- ❌ No multi-location
- ❌ No online orders
